### **Brand Guidelines**

**Logo Spacing (Minimum Clear Space):**
- Leave 20px clear space around logo
- Don't squeeze the logo
- Don't stretch it unevenly
- Don't change colors

**Logo Sizes:**
- Minimum size: 64px × 64px
- Website header: 180px × 180px
- Landing page hero: 300px × 300px
- Favicon: 32px × 32px

**Color Usage:**
- Primary: Use Dashly Blue for main branding
- Never use: Faded colors, inverted colors (unless specified)
- Dark mode: Use white logo on blue background
- Light mode: Use blue logo on white background
